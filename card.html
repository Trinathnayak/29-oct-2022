Vijaya Gurunatha Sethupathi Kalimuthu (born 16 January 1978), known professionally as Vijay Sethupathi, is an Indian actor and film producer who predominantly works in Tamil films.[5][6] He is a recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award. Following a stint as an NRI accountant in Dubai, Sethupathi started his film career working as a background actor and playing minor supporting roles for few years before playing his first lead role in Seenu Ramasamy's Thenmerku Paruvakaatru (2010). In 2012, he rose to massive popularity with critical and commercial successes in Pizza, Naduvula Konjam Pakkatha Kaanom and Sundarapandian.[7][8] Sethupathi has established himself as an actor with his notable work in Tamil films like Idharkuthane Aasaipattai Balakumara (2013), Pannaiyarum Padminiyum (2014), Kadhalum Kadandhu Pogum (2016), Iraivi (2016), Vikram Vedha (2017), 96 (2018), Chekka Chivantha Vaanam (2018), Petta (2019), Super Deluxe (2019), Master (2021), Kaathuvaakula Rendu Kaadhal (2022) and Vikram (2022) and Telugu films like Sye Raa Narasimha Reddy (2019) and Uppena (2021).
